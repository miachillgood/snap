# SeenWords Brand Notes

## Name

SeenWords means words the learner has actually seen in real life.

Short positioning:

> Learn the English you saw today.

## Visual System

- Primary cue: camera viewfinder plus word sticker.
- Core colors: warm purple, soft yellow, clean system white, category accents.
- Shape language: rounded iOS surfaces, capsule word chips, soft material cards.
- Icon language: SF Symbols only, favor photo, viewfinder, memory, stack, and profile metaphors.

## Motion System

- Capture: photo appears first, then a soft outline pulse and scan sweep.
- Recognition: useful words reveal one by one as sticker chips.
- Review: photo context comes before answer reveal.
- Avoid: heavy robot, brainy AI, neon sci-fi, or technical loading language.

## App Icon

The icon combines:

- Purple base for memorability.
- White viewfinder corners for photo capture.
- Yellow word sticker for vocabulary.

This keeps the app icon, scan animation, and word chips in the same family.
